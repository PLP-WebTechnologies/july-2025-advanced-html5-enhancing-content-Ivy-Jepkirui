What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity involves protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. With the increasing use of technology, individuals and organizations must understand how to safeguard their information against evolving cyber threats.

Common Cyber Threats

Phishing

Phishing is a fraudulent attempt to obtain sensitive information by disguising as a trustworthy entity, often through email.

Malware

Malware is malicious software that can damage or disable systems, steal data, or give unauthorized access to attackers.

Ransomware

Ransomware locks a user's files or system until a ransom is paid, often causing data loss and service disruption.
